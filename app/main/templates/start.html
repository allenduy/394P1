Welcome to Dice!
Here's how it works: Enter in the information below and submit the form. It will then take you over to another page in which each player will have an opportunity to get their picture taken! From there, you can select who goes first and what order. Once you start your game, it will cycle through each player or team allowing players to add/subtract points, skip, or end the game. What you can do is assign someone to look over the dice rolling or send the device around for the players to roll their own dice.
Currently only the DEFAULT type of game is available.
